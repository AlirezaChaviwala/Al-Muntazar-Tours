Our Journey

We performed our first journey to Saudi Arabia, Iraq and Iran in 1994 and noticed the hardships that Momineen had to face in their journey. It was then that we resolved to provide comfort to Momineen passengers and make the spiritual journey of Hajj , Umrah & Ziyarah easy. Over the last 25 years and having catered to a large section of our community, we have only strived harder every year to provide better service to the Hujjaj & the Zuwwar.
May Allah(azzawajal) give us the Tawfeeq to continue in our mission with success. Ameen
